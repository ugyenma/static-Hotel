Rules and Regulations
Tashi

Rules and Regulations:


1. Hotel rooms are rented for hotel days.


2. In case the guest fails to appear in the hotel by 6 p.m. of the accommodation day despite making a reservation, the fee for the room shall be charged by the hotel.


3. The hotel guest cannot hand over a room to third persons, even if the period for which the guest paid has not yet expired.


4. Persons who are not checked in the hotel may stay in a hotel room from 07:00 a.m. till 10:00 p.m. Persons staying in a room after 10:00 p.m. must check in the hotel.


5. The hotel accepts guests traveling with pets. Only one pet is allowed per room for an extra charge and the guest bears full responsibility for any damage caused by their pet.


6. The guest should notify the hotel's reception desk immediately upon noticing any damage.


7. The lights-out period starts at 10:00 p.m. and ends at 07:00 a.m. of the following day.


8. Conduct of guests and persons using services of the hotel should not disturb the peaceful stay of other guests.


9. It is prohibited to consume foodstuffs bought outside the hotel in the common area.


10. Smoking in hotel rooms, corridor, staircase, restaurant, bar, spa&wellness zone, cloak rooms, gym and reception hall is prohibited.
